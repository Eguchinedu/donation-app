Who we are

Techaid is a Not for profit initiative created to support young people in Africa starting their careers in technology. According to the International Finance Corporation (IFC), about 230 million jobs in Sub-Saharan Africa will require digital skills by 2030 but currently a huge percentage of the youths in Africa lack access to tools required to develop and earn from these digital skills, these youngsters have drive and the zeal but sadly drive is not enough.

Our vision

A more inclusive and diverse tech community in Africa where anyone can make it in tech regardless of their financial background.

Our mission

To provide laptops and other essential tools to those eager to start their tech journey but are hindered by lack of resources.

We are working to secure the future of Africa by providing as many youths as possible with laptops and other tools including internet subscriptions to facilitate their digital skill growth and career and we need your help.
